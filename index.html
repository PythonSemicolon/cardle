INSTRUCTIONS

General

Every day, a unique car model is selected as the mystery CARDLE. Your job is to identify this car using clues provided from your guesses in no more than 10 attempts!

What car models may be guessed?

CARDLE models must have been manufactured in the US at some point during the past 30 years. Modifiers such as "Cargo", "Sport", "Passenger" and even "Hybrid" and "Electric" have been removed to avoid redundancy and tedious guesswork, even if these names tend to represent significant design differences (and we sincerely apologize if this limitation proves offensive to purists!). For models with multiple editions over the years, the earliest model has been selected. What do each of the squares mean?

There are five squares, each offering unique hints to guide you towards the CARDLE.

  1. Year: The first square's value corresponds to the year in which the model was first manufactured. Green indicates a guess within +/-1 years; yellow indicates a guess within +/-5 years; black indicates a guess off by 5 or more years.

    Exception: 1992 is the earliest available year for this dataset, thus 1992 editions are the earliest editions possible. Older cars may be trickier to identify by year!

  2. Category: The second square's value represents the model's type, which corresponds to at least one of the following categories:
    • SEDAN
    • COUPE
    • SUV
    • CONVERTIBLE
    • WAGON
    • HATCHBACK
    • MINIVAN/VAN
    • PICKUP
    Models with multiple category designations display a generic "Multiple" icon when guessed. Green indicates that the guessed model's category/categories are correct; yellow indicates one or two but not all categories match the mystery model; black indicates only incorrect categories.
  3. Country: The third square represents the country of origin of the manufacturer. A green guess indicates that the manufacturer is from the same country; yellow indicates an incorrect country but correct continent; black indicates an incorrect continent altogether.
  4. Make: The fourth square indicates how close you are to the correct manufacturer. A green guess is a correct make; yellow indicates a make starting with the same letter; black indicates any other make.
  5. Model: A yellow square in the fifth column indicates a model starting with the same letter as the answer. When the model square is green, you have found the mystery CARDLE!

Hover over any icon to display more information about your guess.

Good luck!
